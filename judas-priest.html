Judas Priest

My favorite band, Judas Priest, is a british heavy metal band known to be one of the pioneers of heavy metal. The band was created in 1969 in Birmingham, England and released their first album, Rocka Rolla, in 1974. Their most recent album, Invincible Shield, released fifty years later in 2024.

Their impact can be seen throughout the history of the genre. Many of the names in modern heavy metal were influenced by Judas Priest's innovations: the dual guitar method, the aesthetic of studs and leather, and the operatic vocal style.
